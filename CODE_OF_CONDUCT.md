# Code of Conduct

This organization follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

The short version: be decent. Critique code, not people. No harassment, no doxxing, no gatekeeping. Piracy-adjacent flame wars get closed; what users stream with their own add-ons is their business and not a debate these repositories host.
